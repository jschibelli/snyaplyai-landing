
# SynaplyAI Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction
SynaplyAI is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines the data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## 2. Data We Collect
- Name, email address, and contact information.  
- AI-generated content and user interaction data.  
- Payment information (if applicable).  
- Technical data (IP address, browser type, device information).  
- Location data (if enabled).  

## 3. How We Use Your Data
- To provide and improve our services.  
- To monitor usage and prevent abuse.  
- To process payments and manage subscriptions.  
- To communicate with you about product updates and support.  
- To improve AI model performance and accuracy (in an aggregated and anonymized format).  

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties
- We do not sell your data.  
- Data may be shared with:  
   - OpenAI and Anthropic for AI processing.  
   - Payment processors (e.g., Stripe) for billing.  
   - Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) for performance monitoring.  
- Data will only be shared with third parties in compliance with applicable laws.  

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion
- Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service.  
- You may request data deletion at any time.  
- Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days.  
- Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days for operational recovery.  

## 6. User Rights
- You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data.  
- You have the right to object to data processing or request data portability.  
- You have the right to withdraw consent at any time.  

## 7. Security and Data Protection
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.  
- Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only.  
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for internal system access.  
- Regular security audits are conducted to prevent unauthorized access.  

## 8. International Compliance
- SynaplyAI complies with:  
   - GDPR (European Union).  
   - HIPAA (Health data handling).  
   - CCPA (California data privacy laws).  
- Data is processed and stored in secure data centers located in the United States.  

## 9. Breach Notification Policy
- In the event of a data breach, affected users will be notified within 72 hours.  
- Affected data will be secured and a forensic investigation will be conducted.  

## 10. Changes to Privacy Policy
- SynaplyAI may update this Privacy Policy at any time with 30 days' notice.
